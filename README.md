**SaveMyDay (LMLS — Last-Minute Life Saver)** is an AI-powered productivity web app that actively rescues you from missed deadlines. Unlike passive task managers, it fights back — activating **Panic Mode** with a live countdown and AI-generated emergency sprint plans, and calling your phone via an AI voice agent when you're procrastinating. Powered by **Google Gemini AI**, it offers real-time voice chat, deadline autopsies, habit tracking with a 28-day heatmap, and an AI schedule optimizer. Deeply integrated with Google Calendar, Tasks, Drive, and Gmail, and deployed on **Google Cloud Run** — SaveMyDay turns last-minute panic into a structured rescue operation.
